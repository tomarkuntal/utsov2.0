Our Story

Utsov Inc. is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to promoting the rich cultural heritage of India, particularly Bengali culture, in New Jersey and the surrounding areas.

Founded with the vision of bringing together the Bengali community and sharing our traditions with the broader American society, Utsov has been organizing cultural festivals, musical performances, and community events since its inception.

Our Mission

To preserve and promote Bengali culture and traditions through cultural festivals, educational programs, and community engagement while fostering cross-cultural understanding and supporting charitable causes.

We organize traditional cultural festivals, promote Bengali music, dance, and arts, support educational and charitable initiatives, and build bridges between cultures.

Our Vision

To be the leading organization in New Jersey for Bengali cultural preservation, community building, and philanthropic activities, creating a vibrant and inclusive cultural landscape for future generations.

We focus on cultural preservation and promotion, community building and engagement, educational outreach programs, and philanthropic leadership.

Saturday, October 4th - Double Dhamala

Nandi Sisters

Get ready to be mesmerized by the sensational Indian singing duo who took the internet by storm! Ankita and Antara Nandy are a powerhouse of talent, creativity, and infectious energy that has captivated millions worldwide.

Highlights:

  • From Balcony to Bollywood! Their journey to stardom began with their genius "Balcony Concerts."
  • A.R. Rahman's Choice! Their phenomenal talent didn't go unnoticed, catching the ear of the legendary composer A.R. Rahman himself!
  • Tollywood Debut! They've conquered the digital world and are now making waves in the film industry, marking their Tollywood debut with the hit song "Golemale" in the film "Raktabeej."

The Nandi Sisters are more than just singers; they are a musical revolution. With voices that harmonize to perfection and a creative spirit that knows no bounds, Ankita and Antara Nandy are a force to be reckoned with in the world of music.

Rupankar Bagchi

Experience the powerhouse voice of Bengal, the one and only Rupankar Bagchi! A National Film Award winner and chart-topping hit machine.

Highlights:

  • The Voice That Conquered a Nation! Rupankar isn't just a regional star; he's a National Film Award winner!
  • A Chart-Topping Hit Machine! From the haunting melodies of "Govire Jao" to the infectious rhythm of "Chupi Chupi Raat," Rupankar has delivered a string of unforgettable hits that have become anthems for millions.

Rupankar Bagchi is a force of nature in the Indian music scene. His journey is a testament to raw talent, dedication, and the power of a voice that can stir the soul and get your feet tapping. He's not just a singer; he's an experience.

Sunday, October 5th - Band-o-Baul

Lakkhichhara FT & Raju Das Baul

Brace yourself for the titans of Bengali rock, the legendary Lakkhichhara & electrifying and soulful sounds of Raju Das Baul!

Lakkhichhara Highlights:

  • Pioneers of the Rock Revolution! Forged in 1999, Lakkhichhara didn't just play music; they kick-started a movement, made anthems That Shook a Generation!

Raju Das Baul Highlights:

  • Global Fusion Sensation! Raju Das Baul is blasting the ancient wisdom of Baul music onto the world stage! As the lead singer and Khamak virtuoso for the international fusion project "The Three Seas," he created a breathtaking sound that is both timeless and totally fresh.

Experience the magic when untamed spirit of Bengali rock meets the new-age torchbearer of Bengal's ancient and mystical Baul music.

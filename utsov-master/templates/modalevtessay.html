Age Group Essay Topic Size Specifications
Group I: 6 to 8 years What is your favorite festival that you celebrate with your family? Why is it your favorite festival? Explain with examples. Times New Roman 12pt, single spaced limited to 2 pages
Group II: 9 to 12 years All of you know Ma Durga has 10 hands. She uses her hands to hold different and use them in different ways. Now imagine one fine day you get up in the morning and find that you have 10 hands! What are you going to do with the hands? After your experience would you like to have these 10 hands or rather have only 2 hands? Be as creative as you can. Times New Roman 12pt, single spaced limited to 3 pages
Group III: 13 to 17 years You must have heard the story of Durga Puja, how Maa Durga with her power helped good win over the evil. Now imagine that you get these special powers for one day! You wake up and find that you have been blessed with special powers to do anything you like and whatever you want. What do you think you are going to do? Times New Roman 12pt, single spaced limited to 5 pages

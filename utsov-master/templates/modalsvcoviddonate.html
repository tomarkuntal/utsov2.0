 Utsov is helping with money and supplies. We need your support.

Currently, there is still shortage of oxygen and other essential medical kits for people suffering from respiratory complications due to COVID-19. We have pledged and remain commited to working with several vendors from US and India to send Oxygen Concentrators and other life saving supplies to India. We are working with organizations such as Humanity Hospital,  Mukti, Nayantara, Tapasya and Sanrai International to make this happen as fast as possible.

Utsov partnered with Tapasya, India based NGO and Mukti contributed to support Covid units in different Cities and Villages in North and South 24 Parganas districts of West Bengal by providing several 5 L and 10 L Oxygen Concentrators, and other essential medical kits to affected families due to second wave of COVID.

Utsov partnered with Tapasya and is also currently working with Mukti to provide immediate support to the people/kids who lost their earning members in the second wave of COVID.

We are also in the process of evaluating new organizations recommended by friends of Utsov.

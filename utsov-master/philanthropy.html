Our Mission

"I alone cannot change the world, but I can cast a stone across the waters to create many ripples."

Mother Teresa

No celebration is complete without sharing the happiness with others, especially the less privileged. Utsov truly believes in "We Rise by Lifting Others". Hence, we started our philanthropic journey through several charitable organizations by donating part of our collections from Durga Puja Event. Your generous contributions and well wishes help us to stay in this journey. Let's walk together to make a better future for others.

Our Achievements

Thanks to your generosity, Utsov has supported these causes:

United States

Hurricane Relief

Supported Hurricane Dorian relief via American Red Cross and Hurricane Harvey via Greater Houston Relief Fund.

Cancer Research

Donated to American Cancer Society for groundbreaking research and patient support services.

Hunger Relief

Supported Feeding Florida for Hurricane Irma relief, part of Feeding America network.

Children's Hospital

Multiple donations to St. Jude Children's Hospital for pediatric cancer research and treatment.

India

Flood Relief

Supported AID India Relief Fund and Bharat Sevashram Sangha for Kerala flood victims.

Education

Sponsored tribal education through Nayantara Memorial Charitable Trust for empowerment programs.

Healthcare

Donated to Humanity Hospital providing free medical treatment to thousands annually.

Special Children

Supporting Shrirampur Child Guidance Center since 2015 for children with special needs.

International

Nepal Earthquake Relief

Donated to Nepal Prime Minister's Disaster Relief Fund for 2015 earthquake victims, providing immediate aid and support to affected communities.

Kids Program

Getting kids involved in giving.

Utsov Philanthropy supports kids in fund-raising activities for their schools. Funds go to St. Jude Children Cancer Hospital.

Note:

Limited to 15 students. Contact philanthrophy@utsov.org.

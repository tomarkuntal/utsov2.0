We are thrilled to announce, for the first time in the USA, Raj Barman with his Grand Music Band.
Raj Barman is one of India's current youth icons and a rising star in the Hindi and Bengali music scene. He is an extremely talented vocalist with multiple hindi and bengali playback hits to his name. He is also a talented music producer and a YouTube star with over 5 million followers. He is versatile and able to sing many different genres of music, from rock to pop to folk. Raj has multiple playback hits under his belt and has won the "Best Bengali Playback Singer" in the Tele Cine Awards. Raj has performed the vocals on top hits in multiple Bengali movies such as Noorjahan, Villain, Mon Janena etc. and on Hindi movies such as Badhaai Do, Mission Majnu, Dr. G, Middle Class Love, Om, Untouchables, Sainik etc. Raj's popular mash-ups, cover songs and albums have more than 165 million views on YouTube. Utsov And Philanthropy Trying to make a difference

 

“I alone cannot change the world, but I can cast a stone across the waters to create many ripples.”

Mother Teresa

No celebration is complete without sharing the happiness with others, especially the less privileged. Utsov truly believes in “We Rise by Lifting Others”. Hence, we started our philanthropic journey through several charitable organizations by donating part of our collections from Durga Puja Event. Your generous contributions and well wishes help us to stay in this journey. Let’s walk together to make a better future for others.

Some of Utsov Philanthropy achievements so far. You made it possible.

We are very proud to announce that Utsov for last 12 years has been able to support many organizations in USA ,India and elsewhere. Our support includes education and residential support for less privileged and tribal people, work readiness, medical, social and educational services, COVID-19 and other disaster relief efforts. Utsov is working with the following organizations in the USA and in India at the grassroots level to provide food, shelter, heath service and other necessities.

USA

  • Utsov contributed to Nourish. NJ, who during the coronavirus crisis, offered "to-go meals" and pre-packed groceries to the people affected by COVID-19. Utsov’s contributions also offer housing, work readiness, medical, social and educational services.
  • In New York City, Utsov supported the Meals on Wheels organization who provided home-delivered nutritious and shelf stable meals to the elderly in response to the coronavirus crisis.
  • Utsov supported several relief efforts such as the Hurricane Dorian relief effort, via American Red Cross. Our contributions have made it possible to provide shelter, warm meals, relief supplies, and health services to support and care for people before, during and after this natural disaster.
  • Utsov Inc. has donated to American Cancer Society. In addition to their groundbreaking cancer research, American Cancer Society promotes healthy lifestyles to help prevent cancer.
  • Utsov Inc. has donated to the Hurricane Harvey Relief Fund via the Greater Houston Relief Fund.
  • Utsov Inc. donated to the Hurricane Irma Relief Fund via Feeding Florida. Feeding Florida is a Partner State Association of Feeding America, the nation's leading hunger-relief organization.

India

  • In addition to partnering with Nayantara Memorial Charitable Trust (NMCT) to provide support and donation for education and residential support for less privileged and tribal people over the last few years, Utsov also contributed to the organization during the lockdown imposed because of the COVID-19 pandemic. Additionally, the Nayantara team partnered with Utsov during Cyclone Amphan to support families in distress.
  • Utsov has partnered with Humanity Hospital and provided support and donations for their social welfare drive during COVID-19 and other natural disasters. Humanity Hospital is a charitable hospital which stands by a large section of underprivileged people in India and saves countless lives.
  • Utsov partnered with Mukti, who has been granted recently UN special consultative status. Mukti aims to empower the underprivileged women of West Bengal by providing training and workshops for underprivileged women, adopt branches, supported reusable sanitary pad making training etc.
  • Utsov partnered with Tapasya, an India based NGO and Mukti to support Covid units in different cities and villages in North and South 24 Pargana districts of West Bengal. Our efforts also helped these organizations to provide support to the families and children who lost their earning members during the second wave of COVID-19.
  • Utsov assisted members of our Utsov family in the US during COVID-19. Utsov provided aid and assistance to members of our community in their moment of need in India and in New York and New Jersey.
  • Utsov contributed to AID All India Relief Fund to support relief and rehabilitation efforts of Flood victims in India​. Our contributions have made it possible to provide shelter, relief supplies, health services, and much needed care for flood victims in India.
  • Utsov Inc. has donated to the Kerala Flood relief Fund via Bharat Sevashram Sangha and its head office in Kolkata, India. The Bharat Sevashram Sangha is a pioneering charitable organization serving the country for the last 100 years.
  • Utsov has donated to the Shrirampur Child Guidance Center, in West Bengal. This is an organization with a goal to support children with special needs due to emotional and behavioral disorders and physical challenges.

In addition, Utsov also donates to various causes or individuals depending on need and availability of funds. For example Utsov donated to the Nepal Prime Minister's Disaster Relief Fund to aid the victims of the 2015 earthquakes which devastated the country. Utsov has also made donations to the St. Jude Children Cancer Hospital on multiple occasions.

About. Who we are

Utsov Inc. is a vibrant young organization founded by a group of young (at heart) professionals. We are dedicated to promote the rich cultural heritage of India. We want to remind our friends and families of our roots.

Our journey so far

Vision. What we believe

Utsov Inc., a ‘not-for-profit’ organization registered in NJ, is dedicated to arrange and celebrate religious and cultural festivals of India, for the purpose of upholding the country's gracious values and rich heritage. We believe in our core values of transparency, integrity, fellowship and stewardship.

Objective. What we want to do

  • To promote the religious & culture heritage of India.
  • To celebrate traditional Indian festivals highlighting their richness, here in the USA.
  • To promote the traditional arts, crafts, music and literature of India by organizing concerts, performances, exhibitions and seminars.
  • To periodically raise funds to support various charitable activities that the Utsov decides to undertake.
  • To aid and assist members, their families, and the community at large as and when needed.

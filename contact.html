Frequently Asked Questions

Event Information

When is Durga Puja 2025?

October 4-5, 2025 at the Jo Ann Magistro Performing Arts Center in East Brunswick, NJ.

Do I need to register?

Yes, registration is required for all attendees. Early bird registration ends August 24th, 2025.

Is there parking available?

Yes, free parking is available at the venue.

Registration & Tickets

How much does registration cost?

Registration fees vary by age group. Please visit our registration page for current pricing.

Are dinner tickets included?

Dinner tickets are available for purchase separately during registration or at the venue.

Can I register at the venue?

We strongly recommend pre-registration as space is limited and registration may not be available at the venue.
